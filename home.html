History about Motocycle

The German engineer Daimler manufactured in 1885 the first motorcycle in history with an engine: a two-wheeled vehicle, with a wooden frame, on which mounts a combustion engine whose movement is transmitted to the rear wheel by means of a belt. The engine was located vertically between the two wheels

Suzuki GSX-R1000R

This bike is a Suzuki GSX-R1000R Suzuki has not skimped when it comes to equipping its new super sports bike and in the standard version it mounts a Showa BPF (Big Piston Front) inverted fork, while the Suzuki GSX-R1000R mounts the Showa BFF ( Balance free Front) and BFRC. In addition, both models will mount an automatic steering damper.
